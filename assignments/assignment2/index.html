Cats

Cats are very playful

Cats are beloved domestic animals known for their independent and mysterious nature. Felines belong to the Felidae family and are often kept as pets around the world.

Domestic cats exhibit a wide range of vocalizations, including meowing, purring, hissing, and chirping, to communicate with humans and other animals. Cats are often associated with positive attributes, such as companionship, and are known to have a calming effect on their owners.
